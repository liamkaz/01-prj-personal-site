my programming philosophy

the thing that's always drawn me to coding is the puzzle solving aspect of it. even through hours of work on miscellaneous school or personal projects, i always find myself enjoying the problem solving process, and feeling a level of gratification when i finally solve a problem, one that i don't find in any other aspect of my life. the creative aspects, the ability to lose yourself in the hundreds or thousands of lines of code, and the satisfcation of seeing your code, just words on a screen, come to life in the form of a game, app, or website, is something that i find truly special. i hope to continue to learn and grow as a programmer, and to one day create something that others can enjoy and find value in.
